Discover The College of Charleston

The College is a future-focused, innovative institution whose students flourish and whose graduates are well equipped to thrive in a rapidly changing world. We are the next stop on your journey into the future.

Why College of Charleston?

With more than 140 majors and minors to choose from, you can pursue a program of study that meets your interests and career goals. Here, you'll have the freedom to mix and match classes to create a unique, intellectually stimulating course of study.

Whether you go for a week, a summer or a full semester, you'll return from your study abroad experience with a new outlook. These meaningful, firsthand experiences with other cultures, languages and people will provide you with the global perspective you need for a 21st-century career.

The College's campus is located in the heart of historic Charleston, S.C., which means you'll have every opportunity to try new things. Start by joining one of the 240+ student clubs and organizations.

The College is committed to attracting a diverse and inclusive student body. And it is our responsibility to ensure that all our students have opportunities to engage responsibly with the community and the world beyond.

Get Involved!

So tell us, what's your “thing?” What's your sport, your art, your greatest talent or your latest curiosity? At the College of Charleston, we have an active student club or organization for just about every “thing” you can think of.

Internships give students the opportunity to gain valuable applied experience and make connections in professional fields they are considering for career paths; and give employers the opportunity to guide and evaluate talent. (National Association of Colleges and Employers)

Our sport clubs are the perfect place to release your inner athlete and often make for a great excuse to hang out at the beach. Or, you can experience hands-on media training with CisternYard Media, an organization that controls our campus television studio, radio station, the newsroom and our award-winning literary magazine, Miscellany.
